# Mori Pricing Hypothesis (v1)

## Positioning
Mori = reflective mindfulness companion (memento mori + short daily rituals), between meditation apps and journaling tools.

## Recommended Pricing Tests

### Plan A (mid-market)
- Monthly: USD 7.99
- Annual: USD 47.99 (≈ USD 3.99/mo)
- Lifetime (optional): USD 89-99

### Plan B (entry)
- Monthly: USD 4.99
- Annual: USD 29.99
- Goal: faster conversion in early PMF phase

### Plan C (premium)
- Monthly: USD 9.99
- Annual: USD 59.99
- Goal: maximize ARPU with stronger content depth

## Free Tier
- 1-2 daily reflections
- limited archive/history
- basic reminder

## Paid Tier
- unlimited prompts/reflections
- themed tracks (stoic, mortality, gratitude, resilience)
- deeper analytics (streaks, mood/perspective trends)
- premium audio/voice packs (future)

## A/B Test Priorities
1. Monthly price sensitivity (4.99 vs 7.99)
2. Annual discount framing (40% vs 50%+)
3. Trial type (7-day full vs 3-day fast)

## Success Metrics
- Trial start rate
- Trial-to-paid conversion
- D30 retention by plan
- Refund/churn rate at first renewal
