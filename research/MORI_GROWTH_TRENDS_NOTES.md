# Mori Growth Trends Notes (v1)

## Trends to Validate with Data
1. Rising demand for short-form mindfulness habits (3-10 min sessions)
2. Increased interest in journaling + reflective prompts vs passive meditation only
3. Higher conversion when apps combine emotional utility + identity framing
4. Retention lift from streaks, reminders, and progress visibility

## Metrics to Benchmark (category-level)
- D1 / D7 / D30 retention
- Trial start rate
- Trial-to-paid conversion
- 30-day churn after first renewal
- Session frequency per active user

## Hypothesis for Mori
- Mori can win by blending:
  - existential clarity (memento mori)
  - practical emotional regulation
  - ultra-short daily ritual loop
