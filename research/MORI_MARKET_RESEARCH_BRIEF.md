# Mori Market Research Brief (Thermal)

## Task
Mori: 市场研究 - 目标用户和市场规模

## Objective
Define initial target user personas, market sizing approach, growth trends to monitor, and pricing hypotheses for a memento mori / mindfulness product.

## Target User Personas (v1)
1. **Stress-managed professionals (25-40)**
   - Urban, high-pressure work, wants short daily reset rituals
2. **Meaning-seeking self-improvers (20-35)**
   - Consumes mindfulness/journaling content, open to reflective prompts
3. **Spiritual-growth users (25-45)**
   - Interested in mortality reflection, stoicism, meditation philosophy
4. **Burnout-recovery segment (28-50)**
   - Looking for emotional regulation and perspective practices

## Positioning Hypothesis
- Not just “meditation app”; more like:
  - **Daily perspective companion**
  - Memento mori + mindfulness + short ritual loops

## Market Sizing Framework (to complete with external data)
- **TAM**: global mindfulness/mental wellness app users
- **SAM**: users interested in reflective/spiritual/stoic practices
- **SOM**: English + Chinese mobile users in initial launch markets

## Growth Trends to Validate
- Rising demand for:
  - anxiety/stress management tools
  - short-form habit loops
  - spiritually-adjacent self-help apps
- Retention pattern benchmark:
  - D1 / D7 / D30 for mindfulness apps
- Seasonal uplift around:
  - New year goals, exam/work stress periods

## Pricing Hypothesis (v1)
- **Freemium + Subscription**
  - Free: limited daily reflections
  - Pro monthly: USD 4.99-9.99 equivalent
  - Pro annual: 40-60% effective discount
- Add optional lifetime plan for high-intent users

## Competitor Buckets to Analyze Next
1. Mainstream meditation apps (Calm, Headspace)
2. Stoic/journaling apps
3. Spiritual reflection apps
4. Habit apps with mindfulness modules

## Next Data Work
1. Collect top 15 competitor pricing + feature matrix
2. Estimate TAM/SAM/SOM with cited sources
3. Build persona-to-feature map and launch messaging
4. Recommend MVP scope + pricing test plan
