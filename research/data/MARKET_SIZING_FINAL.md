## 3. SOM - Serviceable Obtainable Market (12-month target)

### Conservative Growth Model

**Assumptions:**
- Initial launch: 2,000 downloads (Month 1)
- Monthly growth rate: 25% (organic + light marketing)
- Trial to paid conversion: 8% (industry average: 5-15%)
- ARPPU: $5.99/month (mid-tier pricing)
- 12-month target users: 8,500 paid subscribers

**Revenue Calculation:**
- 8,500 paid users × $5.99/month × 12 months = **$611,000 ARR**

## 8. Key Insights for GTM

### ✅ Market Validation:
1. **Large TAM** ($8.4B) with **12.5% CAGR** = growing market
2. **Niche positioning** (memento mori + mindfulness) = less direct competition
3. **Lower price point** ($5.99) = competitive advantage
4. **HK + US launch** = dual-market strategy reduces risk

### ⚠️ Risks:
1. **Low conversion rates** in mindfulness apps (need strong onboarding)
2. **Retention challenges** (Day 30 benchmark is 10%)
3. **Niche appeal** may limit scale

### 🎯 Recommendations:
1. **Launch strategy:** HK first (lower CAC), then US (higher LTV)
2. **Pricing:** Test $4.99 vs $5.99 in HK market
3. **Feature priority:** Life Grid (P0) creates emotional hook for retention
4. **Marketing focus:** TikTok wellness creators + Reddit r/Stoicism
