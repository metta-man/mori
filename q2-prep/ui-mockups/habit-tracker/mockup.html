Implementation Specifications

  • Interaction: One-tap toggle (positive/negative)
  • Button Size: 120px height, equal width, 16px gap
  • Positive Color: Sage Green (#788c5d) - success, growth
  • Negative Color: Ember Orange (#FF6B35) - warmth, challenge
  • Animation: Scale 1.05 on select, 2s ease transition
  • Streak Logic: Consecutive positive days, reset on negative
  • History: 7-day trend view, monthly calendar view
  • Feedback: Haptic feedback on selection (iOS)
  • Data: Store: timestamp, type (positive/negative), notes (optional)
