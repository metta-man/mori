## 🎯 Design Brief

**App Purpose:** Memento mori + reflective mindfulness companion

**Target Emotion:** Peaceful reflection, gentle awareness, gratitude

## 💡 Design Concepts

### Concept A — Golden Hourglass (Recommended)
**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Warm cream `#FDF5E6` with subtle radial gradient
- Hourglass silhouette: Dark charcoal `#333333` (top/bottom bulbs)
- Sand: Golden gradient `#D4AF37` → `#B8860B` (flowing middle)
- Subtle glow: Soft cream `#FFF8E7` emanating from center

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Time passage metaphor (core to memento mori)
- ✅ Gold sand = precious life moments
- ✅ Not morbid — elegant and reflective
- ✅ Strong silhouette at all sizes
- ✅ Warm tone matches brand philosophy

**Design Details:**
- Hourglass proportions: 1:1.3 (width:height)
- Sand level: 60% filled (shows time passing, not empty)
- Rounded corners on hourglass frame
- Subtle inner glow effect

---

### Concept B — Sun Dial Circle
**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Deep cream circle `#FDF5E6`
- Outer ring: Gold `#D4AF37` with subtle gradient
- Inner circle: Darker cream `#FFF8E7`
- Gnomon (shadow caster): Charcoal `#333333`
- Shadow: Soft gold gradient on inner circle

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Ancient time measurement = reflection
- ✅ Circular shape = life cycle, completeness
- ✅ Shadow creates visual interest
- ✅ Very minimal and clean

**Design Details:**
- Circular composition fills 85% of icon space
- Gnomon positioned at 2 o'clock angle
- Shadow cast to lower-left (natural light direction)
- Subtle texture on gold ring

---

### Concept C — Life Ring Dots
**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Cream `#FDF5E6`
- Circular ring of dots: Gradient from gold `#D4AF37` to darker `#B8860B`
- Center dot: Slightly larger, representing "now"
- Subtle radiating lines from center

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ References Mori's "Life Grid" visualization
- ✅ Each dot = week of life (80 years × 52 weeks)
- ✅ Gradient shows time passage
- ✅ Minimal abstract design

**Design Details:**
- 12 dots in circular arrangement
- Gradient progresses clockwise
- Center dot 1.5x larger than others
- Very subtle glow around center

---

### Concept D — Candle Flame
**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Dark cream `#FFF8E7`
- Candle body: Warm gray `#888888`
- Flame: Gold gradient `#D4AF37` → `#F2C94C` → `#FFFFFF` at tip
- Subtle glow: Cream aura around flame

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Candle = meditation, reflection, life's fragility
- ✅ Warm flame feels hopeful, not morbid
- ✅ Classic mindfulness imagery
- ✅ Beautiful gradient possibilities

**Design Details:**
- Flame extends 40% above candle
- Teardrop flame shape (not pointed)
- Soft, rounded base
- Glow extends 20% beyond flame

## 📐 Production Specifications

### Base Icon (1024×1024)
- Canvas: 1024×1024px
- Safe zone: 920×920px (90% padding)
- Background: Radial gradient (#FDF5E6 center → #FFF8E7 edges)
- Hourglass: 580px tall × 446px wide
- Position: Center aligned
- Shadow: Subtle drop shadow (y: 8px, blur: 24px, opacity: 8%)

### Color Specifications
```
Background Gradient:
- Center: #FDF5E6 (米白)
- Edges: #FFF8E7 (米黄)

Hourglass Frame:
- Color: #333333
- Rounded corners: 16px radius

Sand Gradient:
- Top: #D4AF37 (gold)
- Bottom: #B8860B (dark gold)
- Transition: 40% → 60% opacity blend

Inner Glow:
- Color: #FFF8E7
- Opacity: 20%
- Blur: 40px
```

### Export Requirements
1. **1024×1024** - App Store (iOS/Android)
2. **180×180** - iOS @3x
3. **120×120** - iOS @2x
4. **60×60** - iOS @1x
5. **192×192** - Android xxxhdpi
6. **144×144** - Android xxhdpi
7. **96×96** - Android xhdpi
8. **72×72** - Android hdpi
9. **48×48** - Android mdpi

### Platform-Specific Notes
**iOS:**
- System applies rounded superellipse mask
- Keep important elements away from corners
- No transparency (opaque background)

**Android:**
- Adaptive icon support (foreground layer + background layer)
- Safe zone: 66% of canvas (center)
- Provide separate foreground (hourglass) and background (cream gradient)

## 📝 Notes for Implementation

**Key Design Principles:**
1. **Warmth over coldness** — cream tones, gold accents
2. **Simplicity over complexity** — minimal elements, clean shapes
3. **Hope over despair** — hourglass partially full, not empty
4. **Timelessness over trends** — classic hourglass, not stylized
5. **Legibility at all sizes** — strong silhouette, no fine details

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Harsh death imagery (skulls, scythes)
- ❌ Overly religious symbols
- ❌ Fine details that disappear at small sizes
- ❌ Trendy design elements
- ❌ Cold, clinical colors
- ❌ Text on icon

**Design System Alignment:**
- Follows 暖极简 (Warm Minimalism) philosophy
- Uses approved Mori color palette
- Matches component styling (rounded corners, gentle shadows)
- Consistent with landing page aesthetic
