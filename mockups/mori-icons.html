🏆 Recommended: Hourglass (沙漏)

The hourglass icon best captures Mori's essence: memento mori — the awareness that time flows, and each grain of sand represents a moment that will never return.

  • Instantly recognizable: Universal symbol of time
  • Warm not gloomy: Cream/gold palette = life appreciation, not death fear
  • Single sand grain: Focus on NOW — this moment
  • Minimal + meaningful: Matches 暖极简 design direction
  • Stands out: Unlike typical meditation app icons (lotus, circles)

Alternative: Life Grid (生命方格)

If uniqueness is priority, the Life Grid icon is distinctive and directly represents the app's core feature. Dark mode with warm dots creates strong contrast on home screens.

Design System Alignment

All icons use the 暖极简 color palette: #FDF5E6 (米白), #FFF8E7 (米黄), #D4AF37 (金), #333 (深灰), #1C1C1E (黑). Rounded corners (24px) match iOS icon guidelines. Simple shapes ensure clarity at small sizes.
